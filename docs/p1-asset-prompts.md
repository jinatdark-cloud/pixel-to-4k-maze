# P1 Asset Prompts v0.1

## Purpose

This file defines the first production prompt set for the P1 asset batch in Maze: Pixel to 4K.

## Shared Style Line

Use this shared style line with every asset-specific focus line:

```text
Top-down orthographic modular game asset for Maze: Pixel to 4K; pixel-first dark fantasy dungeon style; tile-based maze readability; designed for 40x40 px; strong silhouette; restrained palette; transparent background; no text; no watermark; reusable asset, not a scene illustration.
```

## P1 Asset Focus Lines

### Walls

- `wall_stone_01`: Rough gray stone wall tile, chipped stone edges, dark mortar gaps, subtle wear, clearly a solid corridor obstacle.
- `wall_stone_02`: Rough stone wall variant with different block pattern and slightly more edge damage.
- `wall_brick_01`: Orderly dungeon brick wall tile with dark gray bricks and visible masonry rhythm.
- `wall_brick_02`: Dungeon brick wall variant with cracks, darker mortar, and slightly more damage.
- `wall_ruin_01`: Ancient ruin wall tile with old carved stone, broken edges, and faint abstract patterning.
- `wall_ruin_02`: Ruin wall variant with missing stones, deeper cracks, and stronger decay.
- `wall_dungeon_01`: Heavier dark wall tile for deeper sectors, larger stones, oppressive fortified tone.
- `wall_dungeon_02`: Deep dungeon wall variant with darker cavities and subtle iron reinforcement.

### Floors

- `floor_stone_01`: Walkable stone floor tile, flat cracked stone slab, low contrast, clearly passable.
- `floor_stone_02`: Stone floor variant with different crack pattern and quiet surface rhythm.
- `floor_dirt_01`: Muted compact dirt floor tile for cave or fringe sectors, simple texture.
- `floor_dirt_02`: Dirt floor variant with small stone flecks and slight irregularity.
- `floor_moss_01`: Stone floor with small muted moss patches, old and damp but not bright.
- `floor_moss_02`: Moss floor variant with different crack layout and subdued green accents.

### Corners

- `corner_inner_01`: Modular inner corner wall tile connecting two inward-facing walls.
- `corner_inner_02`: Inner corner variant with more chipped stone and worn edge profile.
- `corner_outer_01`: Modular outer wall corner tile for the outside edge of a turning wall.
- `corner_outer_02`: Outer corner variant with light damage and strong obstacle readability.

### Doors

- `door_wood_01`: Sturdy one-tile wooden dungeon door, dark planks, simple iron bands.
- `door_iron_01`: Heavy one-tile iron or iron-plated dungeon door, stronger security feel.
- `door_magic_01`: Sealed stone or metal door with faint blue arcane accent, subtle and serious.

### Mechanisms

- `lever_01`: Small dungeon lever on a stone or metal base, clearly interactive.
- `pressure_plate_01`: Square floor pressure plate inset into stone, visible but believable.
- `pressure_plate_02`: Pressure plate variant with different shape or subtle metal edge.
- `spike_trap_01`: One-tile floor trap with sharp iron spikes emerging from a stone base.

### Treasures and Pickups

- `key_bronze`: Small bronze key with simple silhouette, easy to spot.
- `key_silver`: Small silver key, similar readability but distinct material tone.
- `key_gold`: Small muted gold key signaling higher value or rarity.
- `chest_small`: Small wooden treasure chest with dark planks and simple iron bands.
- `gem_blue`: Small blue crystal or gem pickup, high contrast, clear silhouette.

## Recommended Production Order

1. `wall_stone_01`
2. `wall_stone_02`
3. `floor_stone_01`
4. `floor_stone_02`
5. `door_wood_01`
6. `key_bronze`
7. `pressure_plate_01`
8. `spike_trap_01`
9. `chest_small`
10. `gem_blue`

## Usage

For each asset, combine the shared style line with the relevant asset focus line. Generate and review assets one by one so visual consistency can be checked before expanding the batch.
